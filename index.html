About

Piazza Panic is a game that was initially developed by team 13, and we took on the task of continuing development to turn it into its final product. The game requires you to serve customers before their timer runs out, or else you lose reputation points. We added many functionalities and fixed various bugs to make the game more enjoyable. There are powerups available to help you out, and the game ends if you lose all your reputation points or successfully serve all customers. Additionally, there's an endless mode where you can play until you lose all your reputation points.
